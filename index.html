Poultry Sorting

Poultry Sorting™ provides automated and precise grading of birds from 1 to 28 days, delivering greater efficiency and uniformity for poultry production.

With five sorting categories and detailed gram-by-gram recording, the system ensures reliable data for faster and smarter decision-making. Designed for maximum performance and practicality, it features online integration with AviHub, Wi-Fi connectivity and automatic report delivery via email or WhatsApp.

Its lightweight design and easy mobility, combined with ergonomic handling, provide operator comfort and increased daily productivity. Equipped with electric and pneumatic technology, Poultry Sorting™ processes up to 1,600 birds per hour, ensuring high throughput with a focus on animal welfare and reduced operational errors.
